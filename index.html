Failure on the path to Success

Research shows that 8 out of 10 R&D projects fail and approximately 70% of patent applications are rejected. Due to the variety of problems and uncertainty faced by researchers, this failure issue seems to be very hard to tackle.


Beein.io is looking at this reality from a different perspective.


- As way to capitalize on data collected during R&D projects that failed.


- As a cross-data (data for different sources but related to similar projects) helping companies to reduce their failure R&D rate.


- As a new way to secure IP during the research process.


Problems


  • High R&D project failure rate
  • Research data availability during pre-patent phase
  • Secure IP during the research process

Solutions


  • Cross-data reducing the R&D failure rate
  • A market place for pre-patent projects investments
  • Securing IP using NFTs
